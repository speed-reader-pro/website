The science behind speed reading

Speed Reader uses Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) — a proven technique that displays text one word at a time at a fixed location. This eliminates eye movement between words, which typically accounts for 80% of reading time.

By focusing on a single point, your brain processes words faster without losing comprehension. The Optimal Recognition Point (ORP) guides your eye to the perfect letter in each word, maximizing reading efficiency.

RSVP is especially helpful for people with central field loss or peripheral reading needs. If you need Speed Reader for medical reasons, contact us for a free license.
